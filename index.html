Prototype Scope

  • Detects human pose landmarks from camera input.
  • Uses a rule-based baseline inspired by published repetition-counting pipelines.
  • Exports session data for later labeled evaluation; no formal accuracy is claimed here.
  • Draws video and landmarks in one canvas to avoid overlay misalignment.
  • Does not diagnose, treat, or prevent disease.
